Logans Bakery

Come and try some of our delicious baked goods!

At Logans Bakery, we are passionate about baking delicious treats that our customers love. Our cookies, bagels, and croissants are made with the finest ingredients and are always fresh and tasty.

Our cookies are a fan favorite. We offer a variety of flavors, from classic chocolate chip to more unique options like oatmeal raisin and peanut butter. Each cookie is baked to perfection and is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Our bagels are made from scratch and are available in a range of flavors, including plain, sesame, poppyseed, and more. They are the perfect breakfast option, or can be topped with your favorite spread for a tasty lunch or snack.
